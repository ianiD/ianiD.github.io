Experience

My fascination for making pretty things on the web was sparked at a very young age: I remember making simple websites with amazing <h1> tags as far back as when I was 6 years old.

In third grade I joined an informatics club, within which I learned C programming and a big and useful lot of computer science concepts. The awesome support of the club got me to the national olympiad in informatics 6 times (2012-2016, 2019), where I earned 2 silver and 2 bronze medals.

In mid-to-late 2016 I started taking part in community meetups and had great results in a couple of local hackathons.

In 2018 I helped the Creative Motion team build their website and took on a SysAdmin role, helping them with website hosting, DNS config, and company email.

I've also been passionate about CTFs ever since taking part in the 2018 European Cyber Security Challenge national qualifiers and I'm currently playing with the WreckTheLine team, as well as being the organiser of the weekly CTF-themed meetup in Bucharest: jmp0xc0ffee.
